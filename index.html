Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of jobs do you offer?

We cater to job openings across *all fields* and industries within Mumbai. This includes IT, Finance, Marketing, Sales, HR, Operations, Manufacturing, Healthcare, Hospitality, and many more, covering entry-level to senior management positions.

Is there any fee for job seekers?

No, Career Spur Hub does not charge any fees from candidates for job placement services. Our revenue comes from the companies (our clients) who hire candidates through us.

How can I apply for a job?

You can start by sending your resume to us via WhatsApp using the button on our page, or by emailing it to careerspurhubjob@gmail.com. You can also contact us directly to discuss current openings relevant to your profile.

Do you only operate in Mumbai?

Yes, our primary focus is on recruitment for job roles located within the Mumbai Metropolitan Region (MMR).

What makes your resume service "ATS Friendly"?

Many companies use Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) to filter resumes. Our service ensures your resume is formatted correctly, uses relevant keywords, and has a structure that these systems can easily parse, increasing your chances of getting noticed.
